Guayaquil

Guayaquil is the largest city and commercial hub of Ecuador, located on the country’s Pacific coast. It’s known for its vibrant culture, bustling port, and warm climate. Guayaquil has undergone significant transformation in recent years, with improvements in infrastructure, parks, and the city’s waterfront area, known as Malecón 2000, making it a popular destination for both locals and tourists.
